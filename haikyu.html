Haikyuu in Sports Genre

Haikyu!! is a Japanese manga series written
and illustrated by Haruichi Furudate.

What is the story of Haikyuu?

Hinata Shouyou, a short middle school student,
gained a sudden love of volleyball after watching a national championship match on TV.

What's so special about Haikyuu?

It's mostly about friendship between teammates,
friendly rivalry, and trying to reach a seemingly unattainable dream.

Who is the villain in Haikyuu?

Kuro Einz is the main antagonist of the Haikyuu!! series by Haruichi Furudate.
