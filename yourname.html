Kimi no Na wa in slice of life genre

Kimi no Na wa is a 2016 Japanese animated romantic
fantasy film produced by CoMix Wave Films.

What is the story of Kimi no Na wa?

Two teenagers share a profound, magical connection upon
discovering they are swapping bodies. Things manage to
become even more complicated when the boy and girl decide to meet in person.

What's so special about Kimi no Na wa?

Your Name is simply a movie about that feeling people
