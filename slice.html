Main Characters

The central character of the story

Mitsuha Miyamizu

Mitsuha Miyamizu is the main female protagonist of Your Name. She is a 17-year-old girl living in the rural town of Itomori who yearns for a life in the bustling city of Tokyo.

Sakura Yamauchi

Sakura Yamauchi is the deuteragonist of the I Want to Eat Your Pancreas series and the only friend of Haruki Shiga.

Shōya Ishida

Shōya Ishida is a male protagonist of Koe no Katachi. He has lived a 'lone wolf' life after being blamed for the sole bullying of Shōko Nishimiya during elementary school.
